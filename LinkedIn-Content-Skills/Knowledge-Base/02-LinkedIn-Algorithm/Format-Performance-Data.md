## Carousel / Document Posts

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Engagement rate | **6.60%** — highest of any format | SocialInsider 2025 |
| Impressions lift vs average | Up to 11x | BotDog, RecurPost |
| Engagement vs video | +278% | AuthoredUp 2025 |
| Engagement vs text | ~6x (up to +596%) | AuthoredUp 2025 |
| Best slide count | **6-9 slides** (down from 12-13 — carousel fatigue) | Practitioner consensus 2026 |
| Best slide format | 1080x1350 (vertical) | LinkedIn specs |
| Text vs image carousels | **Text-oriented outperform image-oriented** | ContentIn 2026, PostNitro |
| Performance trend | Stable-to-high, BUT generic carousels declining | van der Blom 2025 |

**What works:** Genuinely unique information, actionable frameworks, step-by-step guides, data visualizations. Text-oriented slides with clear takeaways.
**What doesn't:** Style-over-substance, rehashed tips, decorative slides without insight. Image-heavy carousels without text substance.

## Native Video

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Engagement rate | 5.6% | SocialInsider 2025 |
| **YoY reach change** | **DOWN 35%** (van der Blom), **up to -70%** (360Brew data) | van der Blom 2025, pettauer.net 2026 |
| YoY engagement change | +20% (but on smaller reach) | van der Blom 2025 |
| Usage growth YoY | +69% | Buffer 2025 |
| Native vs YouTube link visibility | +42% | Hootsuite 2025 |
| Vertical vs horizontal | +80% engagement | BotDog, Podawaa |
| Optimal duration | <90 seconds (best: <60 sec) | Multiple sources |
| LinkedIn Live vs standard | 24x engagement | LinkedIn official |
| Hook/teaser in caption effect | +2.7x watch time | Practitioner data |
| **Text-first indexing bias** | 360Brew indexes text better than video — text posts get better semantic matching | LinkedIn Engineering 2026 |

**Specs:**
- Format: Vertical 9:16 (for Video Tab)
- Captions: Required (sound-off consumption dominant)
- Upload: Native only (not links)
- Max duration: 10 minutes, but <60-90 sec performs best

**2026 Context:** Video reach crashed significantly. Best used for authenticity and personal connection, not as a reach play. Carousel consistently outperforms video for reach.

## External Links

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Reach penalty | **~60% reduction** — strongest penalty of any content factor | Practitioner consensus |
| LinkedIn official position | "No penalty if content leads with value" | Sr. Director of Product |
| "Link in first comment" | **Now also penalized (2026)** | Practitioner data |
| Reach change (van der Blom) | +5% (contradicting other data) | van der Blom 2025 |

**Best practice:** If a link is necessary, provide full value in the post first. Link after ~300 characters. Consider: do you really need the link?

### What Gets Downranked (NEW)

| Signal | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| "Great post!" / low-entropy comments | Identified as noise, downranked |
| Pod engagement (coordinated activity) | Shadow ban risk, 60-90 day recovery |
| Comments via automation tools | Removed from "Most Relevant" section |
| Generic reactions without conversation | Minimal algorithmic value |
