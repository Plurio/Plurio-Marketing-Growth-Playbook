## WHAT TO MONITOR

### Weekly Quick Scan (30 min)

1. Open Tier 1 profiles → scan the last week's posts
2. Note: any post with unusually high engagement? What made it work?
3. Note: any new format experiments?
4. Note: any topic shifts?

### Monthly Full Review (60 min)

1. All Tiers → scan notable posts
2. Update `Current-Trends.md` based on observations
3. Add new hooks to `Hook-Pattern-Library.md` if found
4. Update `Anti-Patterns.md` if new failures spotted
5. Add/remove people from the watchlist

### What to Extract

For each notable post:
- **Hook:** What was the first line? Why did it work?
- **Format:** Text? Carousel? Video? Combo?
- **Topic:** What was the core idea?
- **Engagement pattern:** Comments quality? Saves? Shares?
- **Angle:** What made this unique in their niche?
- **Applicable to us?** Can any of our authors use this angle?
