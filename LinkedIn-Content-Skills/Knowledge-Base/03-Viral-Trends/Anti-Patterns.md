# Anti-Patterns — What NOT to do on LinkedIn

> Patterns that kill reach, engagement, and reputation.
> **Last updated:** 2026-02-19

---

## ALGORITHM KILLERS (the algorithm penalizes)

| Anti-Pattern | Impact | Why | What to Do Instead |
|-------------|--------|-----|-------------------|
| **Engagement bait** ("Agree?", "Tag someone", "Like if...") | Detected → penalized | LinkedIn actively identifies and deprioritizes | Ask genuine, specific questions about the topic |
| **"Link in first comment"** | Penalized (2026) | Algorithm identifies bridge behavior | Either embed link after 300+ chars or skip the link entirely |
| **Editing post after publishing** | Resets algorithm | Distribution cycle restarts from zero | Proofread BEFORE publishing. If typo found, leave it unless critical |
| **Posting more than 1x per 12 hours** | Cannibalizes reach | Second post steals distribution from first | Minimum 12-hour gap. Better: 24 hours |
| **Topic jumping** (posting about unrelated topics) | Narrow distribution | Algorithm can't classify your expertise | Stay within DIP Expertise Clusters (2-3 topics max) |
| **Profile-Content mismatch** | Reduced authority signals | 360Brew checks if your headline matches your topics | Align LinkedIn headline with post topics |
| **Engagement pods** | Detected → penalized | Pattern recognition of reciprocal engagement | Genuine engagement with real comments |
| **Automated commenting** | Flagged | Timing and language patterns detected | Manual, thoughtful comments only |
| **External links in first 300 chars** | Reduced reach | Algorithm deprioritizes link-first posts | Lead with value, link later or not at all |

## CONTENT KILLERS (kill engagement)

| Anti-Pattern | Why It Fails | Fix |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Wall of text** | Low readability, high bounce. AI harder to interpret | Short paragraphs (1-3 lines), whitespace, lists |
| **Multiple ideas in one post** | Confuses reader. No clear takeaway | ONE core idea per post. Multiple ideas = series |
| **"Bro-etry"** (single-line dramatic paragraphs) | Actively suppressed by LinkedIn since mid-2025 | Normal paragraph structure. Drama through substance, not formatting |
| **Generic advice without specifics** | Zero differentiation. Feels AI-generated | Concrete numbers, real examples, personal experience |
| **Starting with "I wanted to share..."** | Weak hook. Zero curiosity. Will be scrolled past | Start with a specific, intriguing statement |
| **No CTA** | Lost engagement opportunity. Post dies faster | Specific CTA matched to goal (comments/saves/shares) |
| **Hashtag overload** (>5 hashtags) | Looks spammy. No additional reach benefit | 3-5 max. Mix 1-2 broad + 2-3 niche |
| **Too long without earning it** | Reader fatigue before payoff | 1,200-1,800 chars sweet spot. Longer ONLY if every word earns its place |

## TONE KILLERS (kill trust)

| Anti-Pattern | Why It Fails | Fix |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **"We/our" in personal posts** | Feels corporate, not personal | Always I/my in personal posts |
| **Overly polished AI-sounding text** | -30% reach, -55% engagement when detected | Write in author's real voice with real examples from DIP |
| **Generic motivational statements** | "In today's world..." "Success is about..." — invisible | Replace with specific observation from real experience |
| **Humble-bragging** | "I'm so humbled by..." = transparently false humility | Be direct about achievements or skip the false modesty |
| **"Excited to announce..."** | Overused LinkedIn cliché. Algorithm-neutral at best | Start with the VALUE of the announcement, not your emotion |
| **Adding thoughts NOT from the author** | Violates our core principle: repurposing, not writing | Maximum original content from transcripts/notes |
| **Manufactured vulnerability** | Audience detects fakeness. Trust destruction | Only use REAL stories from DIP Stories Bank |

## FORMAT KILLERS (wrong format choice)

| Anti-Pattern | Why It Fails | Fix |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| **Single image post** | Dead format. Down 30% vs text | Use multi-image or carousel instead |
| **Generic carousel (style > substance)** | LinkedIn targeting low-value carousels | Only carousel with original data/frameworks/insights |
| **Horizontal video** | -80% engagement vs vertical | Vertical 9:16 for Video Tab |
| **YouTube link instead of native upload** | -42% visibility | Always upload natively to LinkedIn |
| **Poll for engagement, not research** | -35% reach. Algorithm deprioritizes | Only genuine audience research. Follow up with insights post |

## STRATEGIC MISTAKES

| Mistake | Consequence | Fix |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| **Not responding to comments in first hour** | -64% total comments, miss Golden Window | Block 30-60 min after posting for active engagement |
| **Posting when boosters offline** | Miss early engagement momentum | Coordinate posting time with EU/US boosters |
| **Same format every time** | Algorithm rewards format diversity | Rotate: text → carousel → video → text |
| **Ignoring comment quality** | Short comments ("Great!") don't help algorithm | Ask questions that invite 15+ word responses |
| **Multiple authors posting same topic same day** | Cannibalization | Stagger 2-3 days between same-topic posts. Use Coordination strategy |
| **No post for 2+ weeks** | Algorithmic momentum dies | Minimum 1 post/week, ideally 3-4 |
