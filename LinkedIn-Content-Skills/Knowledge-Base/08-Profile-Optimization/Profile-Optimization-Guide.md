## WHY THE PROFILE IS SO IMPORTANT

360Brew **cross-checks** post content against the profile (Headline, About, Experience). **Semantic mismatch** between what you write and how the profile is described → **distribution suppression**.

**Stats (LinkedIn data, Rock Your LinkedIn Profile):**
- Profiles with a photo → **3x connection requests**, **2x profile views**
- Verified profiles → **60% more profile views**
- 5+ skills → **2.9x profile views**, **4.7x messages**
- Premium users → **6x profile views** on average

## 1. HEADLINE (max 220 chars, first 60 visible in the feed)

### Formula

`[Role/Identity] | [Core Expertise 1] + [Core Expertise 2] | [Unique Differentiator]`

### Rules

- Keywords from your post topics MUST be in the headline (360Brew Profile-Content Alignment)
- The first 60 chars = what people see in the feed and comments → put the most important things here
- Avoid buzzwords without substance: "Thought Leader", "Visionary", "Guru", "Passionate"
- Update when your content focus shifts
- LinkedIn AI tools in Premium can suggest variants (Marketing on LinkedIn course, Tier 2)

## 2. ABOUT / SUMMARY

### Formula (5 paragraphs)

```
Paragraph 1: HOOK — Who you are and why it matters (2-3 sentences)
  → NOT "I am a passionate..." → "I've spent 20 years building performance marketing teams..."

Paragraph 2: STORY — Key turning point / transformation
  → "After building X, I realized Y. That's why I'm now doing Z."

Paragraph 3: WHAT I DO NOW — Current focus with specifics
  → "At Plurio, we're building AI agents that..."

Paragraph 4: PROOF — Numbers, cases, social proof
  → "100+ clients. $15M agency built. 30+ person AI-first team."

Paragraph 5: CTA — What to do next
  → "DM me if [specific reason]. I share [topic] here weekly."
```

### Rules

- Write in the **first person** (I/my) — "Your story is far more authentic when it's coming directly from you" (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course)
- Industry keywords NATURALLY in the text (for 360Brew and LinkedIn search)
- Format: short paragraphs with whitespace (easy to scan)
- **Over 40 words** — otherwise not indexed in search (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course)
- Update every 3–6 months
- You can include **Top Skills** directly in the About section (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course)
- LinkedIn AI tools can help with rewriting (Marketing on LinkedIn)

### What NOT to do

- "I am a passionate professional..." — generic, AI-sounding
- CV-style: "Managed team of 15, responsible for..." — this is LinkedIn, not a résumé
- Corporate speak: "leveraging synergies to deliver value..."
- Listing skills without storytelling

## 4. EXPERIENCE

### Principle: Experience = Story, not CV

- "Use your work experience to tell a story" (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course) — each role = a narrative arc
- LinkedIn Experience field: **minimum 20 words** for AI rewrite (Marketing on LinkedIn course)
- Career trajectory as a story: what you did → what you learned → how that led to the current role
- **Career breaks** can be included (LinkedIn feature) — normalize breaks (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course)

### Rules

- Current Role MUST be filled in (empty = semantic gap for 360Brew)
- Role descriptions: achievements > responsibilities
- Numbers and results > generic descriptions
- Industry keywords naturally in the description

## 5. FEATURED SECTION

**Location:** right under About. The first thing a visitor sees.

**What to include:**
- The best posts from the last quarter (save-worthy, high engagement)
- Key articles or newsletters
- Links: company website, lead magnet, course, event
- Documents: PDF frameworks, checklists
- Video: key video intro

**Rules (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course):**
- You can reorder (drag & drop)
- Links allow custom thumbnails — for a polished look
- Update regularly: at least once a quarter
- Priority: what you most want a new visitor to see

## 6. SKILLS & ENDORSEMENTS

- **5+ skills** → 2.9x profile views, 4.7x messages (LinkedIn data)
- Top 3 skills are shown first — they're what you'll be found by
- LinkedIn groups them: Industry Knowledge, Tools & Technologies, Interpersonal Skills
- Max 100 skills, but focus on quality
- Endorsements from 1st-degree connections → boost credibility
- Order can be changed (drag & drop)
- Endorsement settings: you can turn on/off suggestions to endorse connections

**Recommended skills for the team:**
- AI Agents, Performance Marketing, Marketing Automation, Attribution
- Data Analytics, Campaign Optimization, Growth Marketing
- AI-First Operations, Product Strategy

## 7. RECOMMENDATIONS

### How to get quality recommendations

1. **Pick the right people** — colleagues, managers, clients, partners who saw impact firsthand
2. **Don't use a generic template** — personalize the request: specific project, specific result
3. **Pro tip for busy people:** Draft the recommendation → "Feel free to tweak to match your voice" (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course)
4. **At least 3 recent** on your profile
5. You can reuse quotes in other sections

### Request format (Rock Your LinkedIn Profile course)

> "Hi [Name]. Having just completed [project/collaboration], I'd really appreciate if you could write a recommendation for me. Understanding your busy schedule, I've drafted a starting point below. Please feel free to adjust it however you see fit. [Draft text]"

## 8. VERIFICATIONS

**Verified profiles → 60% more profile views** (LinkedIn data)

| Type | How | What it confirms |
|-----|-----|-----------------|
| **Identity verification** | Government-issued ID via LinkedIn verification partners | Identity confirmed |
| **Workplace verification** | Work email, Microsoft Entra Verified ID, LinkedIn Recruiter license | Affiliation with the company |

- **Free.** Can be removed at any time
- Not available in every geography (identity verification)
- Path: Profile → Resources → About this profile → Verify now

## 9. CUSTOM URL & SETTINGS

- **Custom URL:** linkedin.com/in/firstname-lastname — clean, for business cards and external mentions
- **Website link:** Add the company site right under the headline (Creator Mode feature)
- **Name pronunciation:** Add audio — a personal touch for connections
- **Pronouns:** Optional (LinkedIn feature)
- **Creator Mode:** Activate for amplified content, Featured + Activity moved up, Newsletter access, Follow link
  - Up to 5 profile topics (tags for what you discuss)
  - Choose the primary content type for display
