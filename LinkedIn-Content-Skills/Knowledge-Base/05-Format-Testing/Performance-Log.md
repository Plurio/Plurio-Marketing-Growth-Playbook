## How to use

1. After a post is published — add a row with basic information (date, hook, format, topic cluster).
2. After **48 hours** — update metrics (impressions, reactions, comments, saves).
3. After **a week** — final metrics + notes.
4. Once a **month** — update the "Patterns Observed" section for each author + Cross-Author Insights.

3. **Update each author's DIP** — the "Format Preferences" section should reflect actual best-performers, not the initial guess.

---

## Anti-patterns (what NOT to do with this log)

- ❌ **Don't draw conclusions after 1–3 posts from an author.** Variance is too high.
- ❌ **Don't optimize for impressions alone.** Save Rate and Follow Conversion matter more.
- ❌ **Don't copy "successful" hooks without understanding why they worked** — voice attribution is critical.
- ❌ **Don't compare authors against each other** on absolute numbers — different audiences, different follower bases. Compare on rates (ER, Save Rate, Follow Conv).
