# Algorithm Intelligence — Template

> Template for collecting data on how your platform's algorithm works.
> Update trends weekly; update fundamental changes monthly.

---

## How to Collect Data

### Information Sources

1. **Official platform blogs** — engineering blog, creator updates
2. **Research studies** — Richard van der Blom (LinkedIn), SparkToro, etc.
3. **Your own analytics** — your posts, A/B tests of formats
4. **Creator communities** — what authors and marketers are discussing
5. **Reverse engineering** — observing what gets ranked

### Update Frequency

| Section | Frequency | Why |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| Ranking factors | Once/month | Change rarely but radically |
| Engagement hierarchy | Once/month | Signal weights shift |
| Content lifecycle | Once/quarter | Stable mechanics |
| Trends (hot/rising/declining) | Weekly | Become outdated quickly |
| Dead signals | Once/month | New anti-patterns emerge |
